How It Works

1. Search

Find professional records by qualifications, readiness, experience, region, and availability.

2. Review

Review anonymous professional records without exposing names, photos, employers, or contact information.

3. Request

Request a Pathway containing only the information needed for the opportunity or review.

4. Approve

The professional approves, modifies, denies, limits, or revokes access.

The Mainline Difference

A resume tells people what you claim. Mainline builds a professional record supported by documents, organization confirmations, and project verifications while keeping the professional in control of what is shared.
